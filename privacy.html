Privacy Policy

Bump LTD (bump.ltd, OneBump) is a subsidiary of Simply Union Ltd, and is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information.

Information Collection, And Use

Bump LTD (bump.ltd, OneBump) collects non-personal information through OneBump. When a server owner or administrator adds our bot to their server, we collect information about the server, such as the server name, description, and other metadata. This information is used to provide bumping services to the server and to improve our services.

We collect email addresses upon signing in to our dashboard. This it to provide additional value to our users, and you are able to opt out of email marketing through delivered emails. We do not collect personal information about users, such as their names, or IP addresses. If we receive personal information from a user, we will not use or disclose it for any purpose other than to provide the requested service, unless we have the user's consent or are required by law to do so.

Data Retention, and Deletion

We retain non-personal information collected by OneBump until it is requested for deletion by the server owner or administrator. Once a deletion request is received, we will delete the data within 30 days.

Information Sharing

We do not share non-personal information collected by OneBump with third parties, except as required by law or to enforce our terms of service. We may share non-personal information with our affiliated companies, including Simply Union Ltd, for the purposes of providing and improving our services.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the non-personal information collected by OneBump, including encryption and access controls. We use industry-standard security measures to protect the confidentiality and integrity of personal information transmitted over the internet. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.

Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies on OneBump.

Changes To This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy or would like to request a refund, please contact us at support.
